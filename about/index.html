About Us

Over the years of doing evangelism in New York City, we have met people from all over the world. There are many ways to evangelize- there is personal evangelism, which is a one on one encounter with people telling them about Christ. There is friendship evangelism which is developing relationships with people and bringing them to a church service. One of the most popular is giving away tracts to someone as they pass by.

What we do is called prayer station evangelism which incorporates all these aspects. It’s personal, it’s friendship, and it’s also giving away tracts and many times new testaments.

A prayer station is like a light house with a big red banner on a table that you can see from a distance. Many times when people have a need, they will come to us. We go to the lost and the hurting who don’t know Christ. We also pray for Christians who are struggling. We encounter all the major religions in the world and will dialogue with them “speaking the truth in love.”

If you have a heart to do this, you do not have to be a bible scholar or a pastor or in leadership. You just have to have a heart for people. This is truly fulfilling the great commission. If you’d like to know more about what we do, please get in touch with us with the email under “contacts.”

In His Grace,
Pastor James J. Sloven
